Welcome to Florianópolis

A Stunning island in South of Brazil where beaches and technology meet.

Discover your favorite experience

Relaxing at different beaches

Find beautiful beaches with clear waters and soft sand. Perfect for sunbathing, or swimming.

Tasting typical food at Mercado Público

Try a variety of traditional Brazilian dishes, including caldo de cana and feijoada.

Sandboarding at Dunas da Joaquina

Slide down the huge sand dunes on a board. Fun for all ages and skill levels.

Kayaking at Lagoa do Peri

Peaceful lagoon surrounded by nature. Great for kayaking, birdwatching, & exploring.

Surfing at Morro das Pedras

Great waves for surfing, as well as a vibrant surf culture with plenty of schools and shops.

Hiking through the lush Atlantic forest

A great adventure for nature lovers and those looking for a quiet spot to relax.
